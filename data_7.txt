This weather dataset was collected in 2 years. It contains various weather features like temperature, water level, fire hazard level, wind speed and more.

water_level: level of water in cm
precipitation: precipitation in mm
temperature: temperature in Celsius
humidity: humidity in %
visibility: visibility in km
wind: speed of wind in m/s
weather: general weather condition
0 - Partly Cloudy
1 - Cloudy
2 - Clear
3 - Rain
4 - Strong Wind
5 - Fog
6 - Mist
7 - Snow
8 - Storm
pressure: pressure in GPa
fire: level of fire hazard
wl_change: change of water level in cm
temp_change: change of temperature in Celsius
pressure_change: change of pressure in GPa
